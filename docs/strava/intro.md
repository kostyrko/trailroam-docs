# TrailRoam for Strava

Browse your Strava activities on a single interactive map. View GPS routes, explore a heatmap of your most frequent roads, download GPX files, and manage everything locally in your browser.

## Key Features

- **Map Explorer** — View all your Strava GPS routes on one map with OpenFreeMap basemap
- **Heatmap** — Visualize route density across all your activities
- **Activities List** — Search, filter by sport type and date, sort by any column
- **Activity Details** — Click any activity to see a speed-colored map, elevation profile, and metrics
- **GPX Export/Import** — Download individual or bulk GPX files, import external GPX routes
- **Local Storage** — All data stays in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded anywhere
- **Privacy First** — No account needed, no cloud sync, no data collection

## Quick Start

1. [Install the extension](installation.md) from Chrome Web Store
2. [Sync your Strava activities](syncing.md)
3. Open the **Map Explorer** to see your routes
4. Browse the **Activities** page to search and filter
